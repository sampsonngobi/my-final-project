PROJECT PROPOSAL: DAILY WORK STATION APP(DWS)

OVERVIEW
The Daily Work Station (DWS) app is exactly what its name suggests: a centralized platform that houses the tools and information needed for day-to-day office work. It addresses the challenge of switching between multiple applications to access the resources required to perform daily tasks efficiently.
The idea was inspired by my own experience in the workplace, where managing multiple tools and information sources can be time-consuming and disruptive. DWS is designed to be customizable, allowing users to personalize their workspace so that their screen displays the tools, information, and resources most relevant to their specific job responsibilities and workflow.

TARGET AUDIENCE
This app is suitable for anyone who works on a computer and uses task managers, note takers, to-do lists, calendars, timers, and reminders, and more. 

MAJOR FUNCTIONS:
1.	Calendar: The calendar will provide a daily and monthly calendar view that is customizable by adding meetings and assignments to your Calendar. 
2.	To-Do-List: There will be a to-do-list that show upcoming task with completion deadlines. There will also be a daily to-do list updated after every task is finished
3.	Clock: The clock will display local time
4.	Time Zone converter: There will be a time zone converter that helps users monitor and attend meetings based on their local time
5.	Simple Calculator: This will help with simple arithmetic calculations 
6.	Notes Manager: Taking notes during meetings or writing down ideas as they come is made easy with the Notes Manager 
7.	Count down Timer: This will help user monitor their use of time. 	
8.	Quick Links Panel: The user can store frequently visited links
9.	Messages Panel: The user will get notifications of emails and WhatsApp messages.
